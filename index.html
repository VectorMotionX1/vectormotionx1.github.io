Vector Motion

Vector Motion is my personal home base for AI, robotics, automation, and intelligent systems. This account exists to collect practical experiments, learning notes, prototypes, and useful links as I explore how software intelligence connects with movement in the physical world.

Mission

The mission of this space is simple: learn by building. I want to use this GitHub Pages site as a lightweight README for myself — a place to organize ideas about artificial intelligence, robot motion, computer vision, automation workflows, sensors, controls, and future AI-powered machines.

AI

Notes and experiments about machine learning, generative AI, agents, reasoning systems, and practical tools that help people think and build faster.

Robotics

Ideas around robot motion, perception, planning, control, embodied intelligence, and how robots can understand and act in the real world.

Automation

Small demos, scripts, workflows, and connected systems that combine code, sensors, APIs, and AI assistants to reduce repetitive work.
